  • Welcome to Junckers – the conscientious choice

    Our story began in 1930, when a young Flemming Juncker united his passion for hardwood flooring with a philosophy of sustainable forestry. Until this day, resource optimisation remains deep-rooted in our DNA and we continue to utilise 100% of the forested timber.

    Junckers’ hardwood floors are based on the traditions of Danish craftsmanship and design, which ensures unique, durable floors of quality.
